IIC CUSAT

IIC SOE, CUSAT was established in the academic year 2021 by the Head of the Institution, Prof. (Dr) George Mathew. The President of the IIC SOE chapter is Prof. (Dr) K.K Saju. In the academic year 2020-2021, we participated in IIC 3.0 and secured two stars.
During the academic year 2021-2022, we actively participated in IIC 4.0 and conducted around 40 programs including IIC-driven, MIC-driven, Self-driven and activities in the theme- Startup Innovation Entrepreneurship & IPR, and secured Three stars. We were one among the selected colleges to conduct the Impact lecture series and successfully organised Four different session under the scheme.
IIC 5.0 is currently running in this academic year 2022-2023. Quarter One and Quarter Two activities are completed. Following are the list of activities done in IIc 5.0.
